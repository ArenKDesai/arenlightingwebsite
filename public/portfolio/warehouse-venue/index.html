Warehouse Venue - Multi-Genre Night

October 20, 2024 · Aren

Venue Details#

  • Location: Converted warehouse
  • Capacity: ~150 people
  • Challenges: High ceilings (20ft), large stage area, mixing genres

Setup#

This larger venue allowed for a more comprehensive lighting package:

  • 4x moving heads on truss (overhead)
  • 8x LED pars (4 uplights on stage, 4 audience wash)
  • Full DMX programming with 20+ scenes
  • Coordinated with house sound system

Results#

The height allowed for dramatic down-lighting effects and sharp beams. Programmed different color palettes for each genre (indie rock, hip-hop, electronic) to match the vibe of each performer.

Setup time: 4 hours including truss installation
Performers: 6 acts across multiple genres
Power draw: ~1800W total
