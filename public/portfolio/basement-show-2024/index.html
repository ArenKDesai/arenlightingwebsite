Basement Show - Punk Night

November 15, 2024 · Aren

Venue Details#

  • Location: Residential basement
  • Capacity: ~40 people
  • Challenges: Low ceilings (7ft), limited power outlets, noise restrictions

Setup#

For this intimate punk show, I kept the setup minimal but effective:

  • 2x moving heads on floor stands (stage left/right)
  • 4x LED pars for stage wash
  • Simple controller with 8 pre-programmed scenes
  • Positioned fixtures to avoid audience sight lines

Results#

The low ceilings actually worked in our favor, creating intense, focused beams during faster songs. Used deep reds and blues to match the punk aesthetic, with occasional strobing during breakdowns.

Setup time: 2 hours
Bands: 3 local punk bands
Power draw: Under 1000W total
