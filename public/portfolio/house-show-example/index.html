Living Room Show - Acoustic Night

September 8, 2024 · Aren

Venue Details#

  • Location: Residential living room
  • Capacity: ~25 people
  • Challenges: Must maintain living room atmosphere, very limited space

Setup#

Minimal setup focusing on ambiance over flash:

  • 4x LED pars positioned as uplights on walls
  • Warm white and amber color palette
  • Static lighting (no movement)
  • Battery-powered backup for one fixture

Results#

Created a coffee-shop atmosphere that complemented the acoustic performances. Kept intensity low to maintain intimacy while ensuring performers were visible. Audience frequently commented on the “cozy” feeling.

Setup time: 45 minutes
Performers: 3 singer-songwriters
Power draw: Under 500W
