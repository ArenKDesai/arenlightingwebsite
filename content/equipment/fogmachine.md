## Specifications

- **LED Light**: Static or flashing, RGB and variants. Non-programmable. 
- **Quantity**: 1 unit

The water-based fog might be difficult for people with asthma or extreme sensitivity to the odor, but it allows DMX lasers to show through the fog (rather than just on the surface they're hitting).
